What is VR and AR?
A brand new future.

VR stands for Virtual reality. VR is a artificial environment in which you experience when you put on a VR headset. You can use a VR headset if you have a computer or a phone. AR stands for augmented reality. AR is an enhanced version of reality. AR can be easily accessed through a phone's camera.

Some examples of VR are from games like "Beat Saber" "Five Nights At Freddy's" and "Tetris Effect" and many more. Some examples of AR from games are "Pokemon Go" "Harry Potter Wizards Unite" and "Ingress". These games are all fantastic and will definitely get you hooked!
